About

I am a cybersecurity professional with over eight years of experience in offensive security, defensive operations, and threat analysis. My background spans both public sector defense work and private sector red team engagements.

I hold a focus on low-level systems analysis — digging into firmware, network protocols, and memory artifacts. I document research rigorously and believe in transparent, reproducible security work.

When not in front of a terminal, I contribute to open-source tooling and write about the broader dynamics of the security industry.
